Welcome to the Anodyne Coffee Roasting Company Rebranding Page.

This website has been designed to display a project based upon the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee class Design and Visual Communication. This project displays a current business and company moving into a new archetype and the design process behind the rebrand. The company I chose to move forward with in this project is Anodyne Coffee Roasting Company.

Anodyne Coffee Roasting Company is a Milwaukee-based roastery business that provides coffee, bakery items, and other roastery items with an intention of an exceptional and satisfactory cup of coffee every time.
